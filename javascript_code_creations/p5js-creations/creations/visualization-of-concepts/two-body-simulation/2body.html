How do you control it?
Pause Button - allows you to pause and unpause the simulation.
Control Planet & Control Commet - allows you to control the direction of movement and location
  • drag body to reposistion
  • click to set direction
Reset Simulation - Resets the bodies to original posistion and velocity.
Pause Planet Movement - Lock the planetary body in place so that it is not effected by the comet.
Planet Mass & Comet Mass - Allows you to change the mass of the comet and planet.
Planet Velocity & Comet Velocity - Allows you to change the velocity in a given direction based on where you clicked for the comet and planet.
